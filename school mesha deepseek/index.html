How UniTrack Works

Implementing our attendance management system is quick and easy with minimal setup required.

1

Set Up Your Institution

Add your university details, departments, and courses to the system. Our team can help with bulk imports.

2

Enroll Faculty & Students

Invite faculty members to create accounts and enroll students through our simple interface or API.

3

Configure Attendance Methods

Choose from QR codes, biometrics, NFC cards, or mobile app check-ins based on your needs.

4

Start Tracking Attendance

Faculty can begin recording attendance in lectures and the data will be automatically synced.

5

Generate Reports & Insights

Access comprehensive reports and analytics to monitor attendance trends and student engagement.
